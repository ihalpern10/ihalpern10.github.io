Picks

AFC

  • Denver Broncos vs. Pittsburgh Steelers

    • My pick here is Denver. While I'm not overly impressed with the Broncos-- perhaps the first #1 seed in history with a bonafide quarterback controversy-- I think the Steelers are too banged up to go into Mile High and come away with the victory. I expect a low scoring affair and I like the Under here.

  • New England Patriots vs. Kansas City Chiefs

    • The Patriots were the NFL's most dominant team during the first half of the season and the Chiefs were the best in the second half. This should be a very competitive game, but assuming that the Patriots have Vollmer and Edelman healthy and ready to go, I expect Belichick to deliver New England to another AFC Championship appearance.

NFC

  • Carolina Panthers vs. Seattle Seahawks

    • What a game this will be! Newton's 15-1 Panthers taking on Wilson's lucky-to-still-be-here Seahawks. I expect we will finally see a game with some scoring and this should be the most entertaining of the weekend. Ultimately though, Carolina has too much offensive firepower. The Panthers will host the NFC Championship.

  • Arizona Cardinals vs. Green Bay Packers

    • The Packers looked great in the Wild Card round, but I suspect a large portion of that had to do with their opponent. It will be a different ballgame in Arizona, where Carson Palmer and the Cardinals await a Packers team that has looked unusually vulnerable this season. I expect the Cards to roll, setting up a 1-2, 1-2 Final Four in the AFC and NFC.
