Top Songs of the Moment

1.

Leon Bridges clocks in with a toetapper straight out of a time machine. Coming Home was the first single off of his eponymous album and set the stage for a national tour that led him to play at The Vic in Chicago last October.

2.

Donnie Trumpet and Chicago's Chance the Rapper combine on this banger to create what might have been the song of 2015.

3.

Sofi Tukker's second single, Matadora, earns this selection thanks to a unique sound highlighted by the pounding beat of the Charango.

4.

Lily Lane drops a very catchy jam with Supernatural, a soul song with devious undertones.

5.

What's a music list without Bieber? Devvon Terrell and Futuristic combine here on an entertaining cover of the Biebs' Love Yourself.
